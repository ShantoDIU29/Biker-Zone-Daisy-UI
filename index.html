Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about ordering, maintenance, and support.

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?
Always wear the right safety gear, inspect your bike before each ride, and practice smooth braking and turning before heading into heavier traffic.
How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?
Replace a helmet after any crash, visible damage, or if it is several years old and the padding or shell no longer feels secure.
My bike has been in storage, is it safe to ride?
Check tire pressure, brakes, chain condition, battery health, and fluid levels before riding. If anything feels uncertain, book a quick service first.
What rules should I follow when riding my bike?
Follow local traffic laws, use signals when changing direction, keep a safe distance, and stay visible with lights and reflective gear when needed.
